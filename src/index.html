PghML

We're a co-learning group open to members of all skill levels. Based in Pittsburgh, we meet most weeks for both presentations and work sessions. Join us on meetup and engage on slack.

Concepts

Don't be intimidated

Machine Learning is a huge subject. Not just in hype, but there are thousands of resources online. Courses, blogs, libraries, frameworks, etc. Where do you begin?

Demystifying ML

ML isn't magic. It's a collection of techniques or algorithms used to make data-driven decisions. Once you have a clear picture of what ML is, you can break down the problem of learning into a series steps.

Get started

Get started with the tools and platforms that make learning & implementing ML an approachable subject.

Implementation

Beginners

New to programming. does not have the math or CS background for ML. Only a basic understanding of ML if any at all.

Professional Enthusiast

May be a professional programming but not involved directly in ML. May have the CS background for ML.

High Skill-Level Practitioner

Has strong CS and Math background for ML.
